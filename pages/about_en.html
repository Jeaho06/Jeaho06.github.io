Cheating AI Omok: About the Game

Game Concept

'Cheating AI Omok' was created for those who are tired of ordinary Omok games. The AI in this game goes beyond simply playing the best move; it uses cunning and unpredictable cheats in moments of crisis, just like a human might. Stand against the AI's unfair advantages and claim victory with your pure skill!

Basic Rules

The first player to get five of their stones in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, wins the game. This is the same as standard Gomoku rules.

AI's Cheats Explained

  • Veto: Once per game, when you are about to place a stone to complete a five-in-a-row and win, the AI can nullify that move and permanently seal the spot. This is the AI's last chance.
  • Place Bomb: On its turn, the AI can place a bomb instead of a stone. The bomb detonates on the AI's next turn, destroying all stones within a 3x3 area around it.
  • Double Move: The AI plays two moves consecutively in a single turn, used to quickly form an attack line or block your threats.
  • Swap Stone: To turn the tables, the AI swaps one of its own stones with the last stone you placed.

Simple Strategy

  • Watch the description log: The AI explains why it makes a certain move. You can understand what the AI perceives as a threat through messages like 'to block an open three attack'.
  • Use cheats to your advantage: If you anticipate the AI will use 'Swap Stone', you can place your stone in a less critical position to reduce the cheat's effectiveness.
  • The 4-3 attack is still a winning strategy: No matter how much the AI cheats, if you create more than one attack route at once (like a 4-3 attack), the AI cannot block all threats and will be defeated.
